Daily reframes

"I am a professional in transition. This job is a temporary platform — not my identity."

"Her criticism is information about her management style. It is not a verdict on my worth."

"I am not trying to win their approval anymore. I am playing a different game entirely."

"The market's opinion of me is the only one that matters. And the market wants what I have."

"I am not on a sinking ship. I am on a ship I am about to leave — on my own terms."

Anxiety check

Is this happening now or am I anticipating?
Most anxiety lives in the future — not this moment
What is my actual evidence this will go wrong?
Anxiety is a story — check the facts
Would a colleague in a healthy workplace worry about this?
If no — it's the environment, not reality
What's the smallest physical thing I can do right now?
Stand up, get water, walk to the window

Crispness practices

Respond to low stakes emails within 5 minutes
Trust your first instinct. Overthinking is anxiety, not intelligence.
Headphones on = focus mode — 2 blocks per day
I am unavailable right now
Ask "what do I think?" before wondering what anyone else thinks
Your internal voice needs to be louder than hers
Gray rock difficult interactions
Short, neutral, no emotional reaction. Bland is protection.

Emergency reset

  • Stop. Close the laptop or put the phone down.
    Right now. Everything else waits.
  • Name what happened — one sentence only
  • Is this happening now or am I anticipating?
  • Her opinion is not the verdict
    "Her assessment of me is not the market's assessment of me."
  • Do the smallest possible physical thing
    Stand up. Get water. Walk to the window.
  • Remember where you are in the plan
    Temporary platform. April is coming. Already in motion.
  • Give today just one job — get through it adequately
    Not fixing everything. Just today.
